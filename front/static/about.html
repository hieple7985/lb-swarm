About

Wikiless is a free open source alternative Wikipedia front-end focused on privacy.

The source is available at Codeberg https://github.com/laboon-org/laSwarmWiki.

  • All requests go through laSwarmWiki, your browser never talks to Wikipedia
  • No JavaScript or ads
  • Self-hostable
